Hi. I'm Hamed Nikseresht.

I am Data Mining consultant at FanTech Arya (FTKA). Our mission is to help companies to transform themselves to become Data-driven organization and creating a smarter business future to secure a competitive advantage. We achieve this by offering high quality consulting and hands-on implementation of all aspects of Data Mining in Iran and the world for whatever your need, be it: • Predictive Analytics • Customer Segmentation • Customer Retention • Cross-Sell Analysis • Direct Marketing Models • Fraud Detection • Churn Analysis • Complex Classification • Multi-Variate Model Building • Feature Extraction • Regression • Time Series Analysis We bring meaning to your data, turn it into information that enables more informed decisions, accelerates innovation and increases efficiency. Recently we worked on a Graph mining project for an e-commerce platform with more than 30,000 users in order to increase the Network effect in their system. At the moment we are working on recommendation systems and Customer Behavior Modeling.
